About Us

Our team has embarked on the development of the Customer Sentiment Analysis System (CSAS) with a shared vision of revolutionizing the tourism industry in Mati City. Inspired by the growing importance of customer feedback and the need for data-driven decision-making, we conceived CSAS to provide valuable insights into customer sentiments expressed in beach resort reviews. This project's inception was rooted in the desire to enhance the overall tourism experience and promote sustainable growth in Mati City's tourism sector. By leveraging advanced technology and machine learning algorithms, CSAS aims to analyze and interpret customer sentiments, empowering decision-makers with actionable data to optimize service offerings and improve visitor satisfaction. Through CSAS, we aspire to contribute to the continuous development and competitiveness of beach resorts in Mati City, ultimately elevating the city's reputation as a premier tourist destination.
